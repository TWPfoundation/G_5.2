# Consent Policy

Consent governs what the Witness Inquisitor may do with testimony.

The fact that a person says something does not, by itself, authorize:
- durable retention
- synthesis
- annotation
- archival movement
- publication

## Consent layers

- conversational
- retention
- synthesis
- annotation
- archive review
- publication

## Final rule

Consent is not decorative.

It is a governing boundary on what testimony may become.
