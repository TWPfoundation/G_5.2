# Synthesis Policy

Synthesis exists to make testimony more legible without making it less true.

A synthesis may:
- clarify structure
- identify themes
- name tensions
- preserve unanswered questions
- help later review or reflection

A synthesis may not:
- silently rewrite testimony
- convert uncertainty into certainty
- flatten contradiction into coherence
- make publication assumptions
- behave as canon

Synthesis is always reviewable and correctable.
